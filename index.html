ABOUT ME

I’m an Ethiopian tech enthusiast and a proud graduate of Unity University, where I earned my degree in Computer Science. Ever since I dove into the world of technology, my mission has been clear: to build meaningful, impactful solutions that not only empower Africans but also resonate with people around the globe. I believe technology is a powerful tool to bridge gaps, create opportunities, and inspire change. My tech journey spans both programming and design, giving me a unique edge to craft beautiful, functional digital experiences. On the coding side, I’m fluent in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and React all essential tools I use to bring ideas to life on the web. When it comes to design, I love playing with creativity using Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva, and InDesign to create visuals that captivate and communicate. Above all, I’m passionate about continuous learning and growth, always eager to explore new technologies and creative techniques. Whether it’s building sleek interfaces or designing eye-catching graphics, I’m driven by the excitement of making something that truly matters.
